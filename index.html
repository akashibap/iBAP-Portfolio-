🔄 iMR Powered Work Flow.
1️⃣
Login & Dashboard
Agent logs in. Dashboard shows assigned meter read list, pending counts, and SOS button.
2️⃣
Select Meter Order
Open the MR list and tap on an assigned meter read order. View account details and meter location.
3️⃣
Navigate to Meter
Use built-in GPS turn-by-turn navigation to reach the meter location. Or scan QR/barcode at the meter.
4️⃣
Capture Reading
Take a photo — AI reads the kWh value instantly. Add Front, Reading & View photos (3 required).
5️⃣
Submit & Sync
Hit Save Reading. Data syncs online instantly or queues offline and auto-uploads when connected.
6️⃣
Report & Alert
Raise exception cases or SOS emergencies with one tap. Team gets notified and response begins.

🚀 Features

Complete communication preference management-giving customers full control over how, when, and where they receive utility communications.

📋
Communication Types
Users control which communications they receive — billing notifications, payment confirmations, outage alerts, account activity updates, and promotional messages.
⏰
Frequency & Timing Controls
Set how often to receive communications — real-time, daily summaries, or monthly updates. Define preferred time windows and time zones.
🌐
Language & Accessibility
Select preferred language for all communications. Enable accessibility features such as large text or voice-based notifications for all customers.
✅
Consent & Compliance Management
Captures and manages customer consent for different channels and types. Maintains consent timestamps and ensures GDPR and CCPA compliance.
🚦
Priority & Escalation Rules
Define priority rules for message delivery with fallback options. Critical alerts such as service outages can override standard preferences.
🔕
Do Not Disturb (DND)
Set quiet hours during which non-critical communications are paused. Configure exceptions to allow urgent or emergency notifications during DND periods.
📱
Device & App Preferences
Manage which devices receive push notifications — enable or disable for specific mobile phones or tablets across multiple platforms.
🤖
Personalization & AI Enhancements
AI recommends the best communication channels and timing based on user behavior. Provides proactive alerts such as predicting high bills.
📜
Audit & History
Complete history of all communications sent, along with records of preference and consent changes. Ensures transparency and delivery tracking.
