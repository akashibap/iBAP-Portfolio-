FIELD-READY SMART TOOLS

🤖
AI Meter Reading
Take a photo of any meter display. AI OCR engine instantly extracts the kWh value — shown on screen for confirmation before saving. No manual typing.
📍
GPS Navigation
Built-in GPS navigation guides field agents turn-by-turn to each meter location. Integrated map view with route optimization.
📷
QR & Barcode Scanning
Scan meter QR codes or barcodes to instantly identify accounts. Faster meter lookup without manual account number entry.
☁️
Offline Sync
Works fully offline. Readings, photos and form data save locally. When connectivity returns, everything auto-syncs. Last sync time shown in dashboard.
🚨
SOS Emergency Alerts
One-tap SOS button on any screen. Emergency assigned Notification ID, tracked with due date. Team gets alerted immediately for rapid response.
🔔
Smart Notifications
Real-time alerts for every important event — SOS, meter readings, ticket updates, sync status, login activity, and field exceptions. All categorized.
🔄 Workflow

HOW IT WORKS

1
Login & Dashboard
Agent logs in. Dashboard shows assigned meter read list, pending counts, and SOS button.
2
Select Meter Order
Open the MR list and tap on an assigned meter read order. View account details and meter location.
3
Navigate to Meter
Use built-in GPS turn-by-turn navigation to reach the meter location. Or scan QR/barcode at the meter.
4
Capture Reading
Take a photo — AI reads the kWh value instantly. Add Front, Reading & View photos (3 required).
5
Submit & Sync
Hit Save Reading. Data syncs online instantly or queues offline and auto-uploads when connected.
6
Report & Alert
Raise exception cases or SOS emergencies with one tap. Team gets notified and response begins.

⚙️ Preference Management

ACCOUNT PREFERENCE FEATURES

Complete communication preference management — giving customers full control over how, when, and where they receive utility communications.

📡
Communication Channels
Customers choose preferred channels — email, SMS, push notifications, automated calls, messaging apps, or paper mail. Define primary and secondary channels to ensure reliable delivery of important messages.
📋
Communication Types
Users control which communications they receive — billing notifications, payment confirmations, outage alerts, account activity updates, and promotional messages. Opt in or out of specific categories.
⏰
Frequency & Timing Controls
Set how often to receive communications — real-time, daily summaries, or monthly updates. Define preferred time windows and time zones to avoid messages at inconvenient times.
🌐
Language & Accessibility
Select preferred language for all communications. Enable accessibility features such as large text or voice-based notifications, ensuring inclusivity for all customers.
✅
Consent & Compliance Management
Captures and manages customer consent for different channels and types. Maintains consent timestamps and ensures GDPR and CCPA compliance.
📇
Contact Details Management
Manage multiple contact details — email addresses and phone numbers — mapped to specific communication channels. Ensures each communication type reaches the right contact point.
🚦
Priority & Escalation Rules
Define priority rules for message delivery with fallback options if the primary channel fails. Critical alerts such as service outages can override standard preferences for timely communication.
🔕
Do Not Disturb (DND)
Set quiet hours during which non-critical communications are paused. Configure exceptions to allow urgent or emergency notifications during DND periods.
📱
Device & App Preferences
Manage which devices receive push notifications — enable or disable for specific mobile phones or tablets. Tailors the notification experience across multiple platforms.
🤖
Personalization & AI Enhancements
AI recommends the best communication channels and timing based on user behavior. Provides proactive alerts such as predicting high bills or unusual usage patterns.
📜
Audit & History
Complete history of all communications sent, along with records of preference and consent changes. Ensures transparency and helps track delivery status and past interactions.
